Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Shrimp Facts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you subscribe to our SMS-based shrimp facts service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the following information:

  • Phone Number: Your mobile phone number to send you weekly shrimp facts via SMS.
  • Subscription Status: Whether you are opted in or opted out of our service.
  • Timestamps: The date and time you subscribed or updated your subscription status.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used solely for the following purpose:

  • Sending you weekly shrimp facts via SMS
  • Processing opt-in and opt-out requests

We will never:

  • Sell your phone number to third parties
  • Share your phone number with anyone else
  • Use your phone number for marketing purposes beyond this service
  • Send you spam or unsolicited messages

4. Data Storage and Security

Your phone number is stored securely in our database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Twilio, a third-party SMS service provider, to deliver messages to you. Twilio's use of your information is governed by their own privacy policy, which can be found at https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt Out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe at any time
  • Request Deletion: Contact us to request deletion of your phone number from our database
  • Access Your Data: Request confirmation of what information we have about you

7. Data Retention

We retain your phone number and subscription status as long as you remain subscribed. If you opt out, your phone number is marked as opted-out in our system to prevent accidental re-subscription, but you may request complete deletion at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at: https://github.com/logalleon/shrimp-facts/issues
